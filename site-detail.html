About this site

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel located in the Eastern Cordillera of southern Peru. It is situated on a mountain ridge at 2,430 meters above sea level.

Built around 1450 AD, this archaeological wonder demonstrates the extraordinary architectural and engineering skills of the Inca civilization, harmoniously integrated with the natural landscape.
